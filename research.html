Research

Research verticals
  1. Data-driven closure modeling for Reynolds-Averaged Navier-Stokes and large eddy simulations of turbulence.
  2. Reduced-order modeling for advective partial differential equations using data-driven compression and time-series evolution techniques.
  3. Scalable integration of data science tools within scientific computing workflows.
  4. Interpretable machine learning for fluid flow reconstruction and model order reduction.
