Romit Maulik is an Assistant Professor in the College of Information Sciences and Technology at Pennsylvania State University. He is also a co-hire in the Institute for Computational and Data Sciences at Penn State. He obtained his PhD in Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering at Oklahoma State University in 2019 and has been the Margaret Butler Postdoctoral Fellow as well as an Assistant Computational Scientist at Argonne National Laboratory before starting his current position in August 2023. His research has been funded by the DOE, NSF and Argonne and Los Alamos National Laboratories and he is also a member of RAPIDS, a DOE SCIDAC Institute for Artificial Intelligence. His group researches high-performance scientific machine learning algorithm development with applications to various dynamical systems.
